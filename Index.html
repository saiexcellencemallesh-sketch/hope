February

Sant Tulsidas

The Turning Point

Before his sainthood, Tulsidas was blinded by attachment to his wife, Ratnavali. One night, he crossed a flooded river in the dark just to see her. Ratnavali, seeing his obsession, delivered a spiritual shock:

"If you loved the Divine with the same intensity you feel for this body of flesh and bone, you would be realized in an instant."

This sentence redirected his worldly passion into a divine fire for Sri Rama, leading to the creation of the Ramcharitmanas.
